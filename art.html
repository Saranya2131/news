The Times Of India, Mumbai
Tuesday, October 1, 2024

TIMES OF CITY

From 150 to 67:WR to reduce 
  cancellation of locals from Oct 2.


Mumbai:

After deciding to cancel 150 local services from Monday till Oct 4 due to a 30kmph speed restriction being imposed on all four lines between Ram Mandir and Malad stations owing to ongoing work on the fifth and sixth lines,Western Railways(WR) has slashed the number of cancellations to 67 from Wednesday. Besides, it said,normal operations will be completely restored on Friday.
